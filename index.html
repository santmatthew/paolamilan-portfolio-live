About the Artist

Paola Milán is a visual artist who works from the body, gesture, and intuition. After more than a decade in the world of graphic design, she chose to return to what had always been essential: painting. Her artistic practice is closely connected to movement, well-being, and a conscious way of inhabiting time. She lives and works in Madrid.
